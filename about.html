About Australian Red Ochre

I have always been a arty / crafty person, and have only just started leatherwork in 2015. I do this in my spare time and love doing it.  I have made a lot of items and given them away, so I need to start selling my leather pieces so I can buy more leather....it's kind of an addiction now :)

The name   "Australian Red Ochre"  is a little bit of a mix of special things to me.  "Australian"  because
I am a Aussie girl through & through, the products I make are made here in the Whitsundays Australia.  "Red"  being the colour of this great land and also the colour of my beautiful Australian Cattle Dog, & Ochre, well, that's her name :)  Put them all together & we have
"Australian Red Ochre"

I'm not the best at taking photos, and quite often the photos don't do the leather work justice (not bragging).  I'll get better at taking photos....that's my next thing to learn...photography :)

Leather has a natural beauty and character, no two pieces are the same making each piece of leather individual . Leather is a natural material and can carry, scars, insect bites and natural markings. Treat these markings as its built-in Certificate of Authenticity &appreciate its unique characteristics.

I don't put prices on my page because everything I make is different & unique. Pieces are priced dependant on how much detail goes into each piece and each piece is only made once.  Everything is cut from quality Veg tan leather and hand tooled / carved & hand stitched.

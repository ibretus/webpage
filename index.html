“About the types of leather & materials used by Australian Red Ochre Custom Leather” Australian Red ochre Custom Leather uses Vegetable Tanned Cow hides, kangaroo & pig skin. Unless otherwise stated or requested, all Australian Red Ochre wallets are constructed with cow leather weighing (“thickness”) 5.0 oz – 6.0 oz (2.0 mm – 2.4 mm). Kangaroo leather is used for all insides pockets of wallets & phone wallets. Phone cover outer are constructed from heavier leather for durability and extra protection for your phone. We use Stainless Steel or Brass for all our dog collars All leather products are hand cut, hand carved & hand sewn.
